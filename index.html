About Us

Welcome to Mud of joy + Burger India

Mud of joy X Burger India is nestled in the heart of Ashiyana, Lucknow! We take immense pride in offering a diverse menu of high-quality Indian fast food and authentic cuisine. Our passion lies in bringing the rich tapestry of Indian flavors to your plate.

At Mud of joy X Burger India, we're committed to sourcing the freshest ingredients and using traditional cooking techniques to ensure each dish embodies the essence of India's culinary heritage. From beloved street food classics to aromatic curries, our menu caters to all tastes.

Our cozy and welcoming ambiance provides the perfect setting for you to savor your meal and create cherished memories with loved ones. Join us for a culinary journey that captures the vibrant and diverse spirit of India's food culture.

Come hungry, leave happy, and experience the magic of Indian cuisine at Mud of joy X Burger India in Ashiyana, Lucknow. We look forward to serving you soon!
